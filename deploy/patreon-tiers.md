# QORM Patreon tiers — copy-paste

**Community** is the free default — no Patreon needed. Anyone may use QORM for
personal, educational, and open-source projects at no cost, and build on the MIT
source with attribution. Create the three paid tiers below (low to high price).

---

## Tier 1 — Indie

**Price:** $1 / month

**Description**
For a solo developer or freelancer shipping a commercial app built with QORM.
The price of nothing, so individual commercial use stays fair.

**Benefits**
- Commercial white-labeling: ship your app with your own icon (replace the QORM logo)
- Remove the "Made with QORM" note from your app's packaging metadata
- Commercial resale: sell the product you build with QORM
- Your name on the QORM supporters page

---

## Tier 2 — Supporter

**Price:** $3 / month

**Description**
Back QORM's development. No commercial white-labeling here — this tier is for
people who want to support the project and help steer what comes next.

**Benefits**
- Priority feature requests: your reasonable requests get prioritized
- Your name on the QORM supporters page
- Our thanks for keeping QORM free and open for everyone

---

## Tier 3 — Studio

**Price:** $7 / month

**Description**
For a company or team shipping a commercial product built with QORM.

**Benefits**
- Commercial white-labeling for your company: your own app icon
- Remove the "Made with QORM" note from packaging metadata
- Commercial resale for your business
- Priority feature requests
- Your company on the QORM supporters page

---

*Icon change and removing the "Made with QORM" note are commercial white-labeling
(Indie or Studio). Packaging with a custom icon or `--no-branding` asks you to
confirm your membership — honour-system, no verification. See TERMS.md.*
